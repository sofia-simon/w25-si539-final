Searching for Job and/or Internship Postings

Overview

There are several different places to search for job/internship postings. In this page, we'll highlight some of the most common places UMSI students find opportunities.

CareerLink

UMSI CareerLink offers on-demand access to career support: internships, jobs, career advising, career-building resources, special events and more. This online tool is exclusively for enrolled UMSI students and alumni.

Some of the most important features to know on CareerLink to help you search for job/internship postings include:

  • OCI and Job Listings Tab - Go here to search for opportunities. OCI stands for on-campus interviews.
  • Filters - Use filters to narrow down your search. A favorite is the "Job Function" filter (e.g., UX Design, Library Science).
  • Email Alerts - At the bottom left corner of the listings page, turn on alerts for new opportunities that match your filters.
