Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Office (CDO)

At the School of Information, your career journey matters — and we're here to support you every step of the way.

Whether you're exploring new opportunities, refining your professional goals, or actively applying for jobs and internships, the UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) is your partner in success. Our CDO site is your go-to hub for connecting with career coaches, accessing tailored resources, and staying informed about events and opportunities designed with your future in mind.

Please let us know if you have questions about the content of this site, and how we can ensure CDO services, resources and events are inclusive to you. What accommodation or access needs can we facilitate? Contact umsi.careers@umich.edu
